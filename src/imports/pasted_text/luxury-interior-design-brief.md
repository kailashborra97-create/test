Luxury Interior Design Website – Dark & Gold Theme

Create a modern, luxury interior design company website with dark background and gold accent theme, inspired by high-end brands and platforms like HomeLane but with original layout and content.

The design should feel premium, elegant, spacious, and modern with strong visual hierarchy and smooth UI animations.

Use dark charcoal / black background with gold accents and minimal white text.
